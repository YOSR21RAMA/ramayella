Pigmentation is no more with this trio set from SVR! The Pigmentation & Radiance Set reduces stubborn dark spots, blemish marks, sun damage and uneven skin tone to reveal a luminous complexion.

The Pigmentation & Radiance Set includes the SVR Ampoule [C] Anti-Ox with 20% stabilised Vitamin C to protect the skin from environmental damage, boost collagen production and restore radiance. Another clarity booster, the SVR CLAIRIAL Pigmentation & Dark Spot Serum, improves uneven skin on the face, neck, décolletage and hands. And finally, end your night using the SVR CLAIRIAL Night Peel Brush Applicator - an innovative overnight peeling treatment that participants trialled and found in 14 days, their dark spots faded by 20%!

Le Benefits
  • 1x SVR Ampoule [C] Anti-Ox 30ml
  • 1x SVR CLAIRIAL Pigmentation & Dark Spot Serum 30ml
  • 1x SVR CLAIRIAL Night Peel Brush Applicator 50ml
Pour Qui

Suitable for all skin types

Apply SVR's CLAIRIAL Pigmentation & Dark Spot Serum in the morning and/or evening before your moisturiser.


Apply SVR's Ampoule [C] Anti-Ox morning and night before your normal cream or mix directly into your cream. Be sure to avoid eye contours.


Apply SVR's CLAIRIAL Night Peel with a brush in a thin layer during night-time skincare routine. In the event of irritation (tingling, overheating, etc), space out applications. Avoid the eye contour.
